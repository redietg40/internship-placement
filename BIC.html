Our Vision & Mission

About Bahir Dar Incubation Center

The Bahir Dar Incubation Center (BIC) vision is to build a secure, advanced, and accessible technological capacity to realize the region's economic growth by 2042. Its mission is to identify, adapt, develop, and transfer effective technologies to accelerate this progress. Its operational values emphasize deep observation, creating impact, honesty, continuous learning, innovation, and collaboration.

The Bureau's extensive functions can be summarized into several key areas: Policy & Implementation, where it aligns national science, technology, and innovation policies with the region's specific needs and implements them. Digital Transformation focuses on building digital infrastructure, promoting digital literacy, and supporting the adoption of emerging technologies, ensuring equitable access to technology through community centers, and supporting the digitalization of government services to make them more efficient. We also focus on supporting startups with funding and workspace, encouraging public-private partnerships, and facilitating research and development, especially in emerging fields like biotechnology.

Knowledge & Data Management is another crucial area where the Bureau establishes a centralized regional data center, develops frameworks for data and intellectual property management, and protects indigenous knowledge and genetic resources. Security & Standards involves creating a cybersecurity center to protect regional data and networks, while also setting and enforcing technology standards. Inclusion & Capacity Building commits to promoting the participation of women, youth, and persons with disabilities in the tech sector and focuses on building the capacity of its own staff and the broader community.

Ultimately, the Bureau acts as the central coordinating body for all technology-related activities in the region. It is designed to be a facilitator, regulator, and bridge between researchers, industries, educational institutions, and the public to ensure technology is used as a powerful and inclusive tool for the region's comprehensive development. It is established as a legal entity with the power to sue, be sued, and enter into contracts.

About BIC

Transforming Ideas into Reality

We're more than just an incubation center - we're a catalyst for innovation and entrepreneurship in Ethiopia.

Innovation Hub

Bahir Dar Incubation Center (BIC) is a leading innovation hub in Ethiopia, dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship and technological innovation. Established in 2018, we have successfully incubated over 150 startups and provided mentorship to thousands of aspiring entrepreneurs.

Perfect for All Experience Levels

BIC welcomes students from diverse backgrounds - whether you're a seasoned professional with extensive work experience, a student with foundational knowledge, or a complete beginner eager to learn. Our innovative approach leverages cutting-edge AI technologies and intelligent systems to provide personalized learning experiences tailored to your skill level.

What sets us apart is our emphasis on effective communication with advanced technologies. Students learn not just to use AI tools, but to master the art of prompt engineering, data interpretation, and result optimization.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a thriving ecosystem where innovative ideas can flourish, connecting startups with investors, mentors, and industry experts. We specialize in technology-driven solutions that address local and global challenges.
